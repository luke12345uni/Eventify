About Eventify

A platform built for real event organisers

Eventify is designed with the goal of creating,managing and executing events from start to finish within one centralised platform, empowering the organiser with complete control.

The platform follows the MVC (Model-View-Controller) architectural pattern, separating data, business logic and presentation into clean, maintainable layers. All data can be viewed,edited or deleted with ease.

Designed with stric adherence with the 960 Grid System, the Rule of Thirds layout principle, and Nielsen's 10 Usability Heuristics — providing the user with a clear, effective experiance.

Design Principles Applied
For a well structed design, this web platform complies with these three design principles:
960 Grid System
All layouts constrained to a 960px max-width container with consistent 24px padding for reliable alignment across all pages and adhearance to the 960 grid layout.
Nielsen's 10 Heuristics.
For maximum usability, visibility of status, error recovery, consistency and minimal design applied throughout the UI
Rule of Thirds
Hero top section and About sections use a 2:1 column split, prioritising primary content in the first two thirds, therefore placing supporting visuals in the third for a graphically pleasing design.
